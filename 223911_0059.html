punishment visited on an Austrian playwright, Arthur Snitch¬

dueling. The dispatch says he has been expelled from the ranks
of the reservist officers in the Austrian army, and infamously de¬
graded by a military court of honor. That sounds very barbarous
10 us; but barbarism in one country seems to be civilization in
another. A good deal, after all, depends upon the point of view.
